Sydney Page Rahilly


Sydney Page Rahilly is an American actress originally from Boston, Massachusetts. With her passion for the arts starting when she was five, she has pursued her career by moving to Los Angeles, California, where she is working towards her BFA in Acting at the California Institute of the Arts. Alongside of her training, she enjoys indulging in her other interests such as modeling, dancing, exploring new places and cultures by traveling both locally and abroad, and cooking a nice meal for her friends and family. Her most notable work is her role of Amy in the feature film, "Minor Leaguer" (2024).
